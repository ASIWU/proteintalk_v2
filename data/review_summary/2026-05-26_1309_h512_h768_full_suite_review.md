## Conclusion
h512 should remain the default if a single parameter size must be selected for all exp_01 to exp_08. h768 improves unseen cell type by +0.02012 AUPRC and unseen cell by +0.01100 AUPRC, but it reduces the primary single unseen drug AUPRC by -0.01706, slightly reduces double unseen drug pair AUPRC, weakens the MSE-gap signal, and takes about 19% longer wall time on the 2-GPU standard suite.
